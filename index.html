Our Heritage

Our Story

Crave Mediterranean Grill is a family-owned restaurant in the heart of Glendale, Colorado, bringing over 20 years of Mediterranean and Lebanese culinary tradition to the Denver metro area. Every dish starts with the freshest ingredients and our treasured family recipes — from hand-rolled grape leaves to wood-fired pies and slow-marinated kabobs.

We don't just serve food. We share a piece of our heritage with every plate.
